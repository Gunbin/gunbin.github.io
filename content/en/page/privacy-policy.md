# Privacy Policy

TrendRadar ("this blog") handles only necessary information and strives to comply with relevant laws and platform policies.

## Information That May Be Processed

This blog does not run its own user registration system.  
However, the following information may be processed automatically during normal site usage:

- Browser type, device details, IP, and access time
- Visited pages, time on page, and referral paths in aggregated form
- Identifiers handled by third-party tools for comments or embeds

## Why We Use This Information

- To keep the site secure and stable
- To improve content quality and user experience
- To understand traffic patterns and performance
- To support ad delivery and measurement where applicable

## Cookie Usage

This blog may use cookies for analytics and advertising.

- Audience and traffic analytics
- Content and ad personalization
- Service usage pattern analysis

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings.  
Some site features may not function as intended if cookies are disabled.

## Third-Party Services

This blog may rely on third-party providers, such as:

- Web analytics services
- Advertising platforms (for example, Google AdSense)
- Commenting or social sharing tools

Each third-party service processes data under its own privacy policy.

## Policy Updates

This policy may be revised when laws, tools, or operating practices change.  
Any updates will be reflected on this page.
